# Civic Routing Knowledge for Delhi

## Department Mapping
- **MCD (Municipal Corporation of Delhi)**: Handles issues related to potholes, garbage/sanitation, streetlights, illegal construction, and stray animals.
- **Delhi Jal Board (DJB)**: Handles issues related to water leakage.
- **Local Discom (BSES/Tata Power)**: Handles electricity and power supply issues.
- **DPCC (Delhi Pollution Control Committee)**: Handles open garbage burning, construction dust complaints, industrial smoke, and visible vehicle smoke (classified under "pollution/air quality" category).
- **CPGRAMS (Centralized Public Grievance Redress and Monitoring System)**: The escalation path for unresolved complaints after several days.

## Complaint Channels & Formats
- **MCD 311 App**: The primary channel for logging MCD complaints. Reports should be concise and must include the category and a photo if available.
- **Delhi Jal Board portal/helpline**: The primary channel for DJB complaints. Reports should include the exact location and description of the leak.
- **BSES/Tata Power consumer portal**: The primary channel for power issues. Reports require the CA (Consumer Account) number or exact pole number/address.
- **DPCC portal/helpline**: The primary channel for DPCC complaints. Complaints can be filed via dpcc.delhigovt.nic.in or DPCC helpline 1800-11-4000. Reports should include the exact location, description, and details of the air/pollution violation.
- **CPGRAMS portal**: Used for escalation. Provide the previous complaint reference numbers and history of no resolution.
